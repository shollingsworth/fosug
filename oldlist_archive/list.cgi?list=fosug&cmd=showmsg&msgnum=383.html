I've been playing around with this distro, I have it installed on my 
Mother's computer too.  It's not too bad.  There's several things 
lacking from the base install, so it will be a good platform to show how 
to install stuff.  I would recommend that you download some of the 
packages before hand, just to save time downloading durring your 
presentation.  In the package manager, it gives you the option to 
download only.

Seeing as I like KDE over Gnome-- Just a personal prefrence, I installed 
KDE.  Also, if you uncomment out the "world" listings in the 
/etc/apt/sources.list you'll have a lot more programs available to you 
(such as KDE.)

Anyway, just my input on this.
