This needs some work but its a different take on the mission
statement.  The classic mission statement is very bland and just
proclaims "we are great and we will be better".  They are frequently
edited by lawyers and boards of directors.  The only thing missing is
a blessing from both political parties.  We need something for real
humans.  Food for thought:

Fresno Open Source User Group Mission

There's a lot more to Open Source than free software.  Open Source is
about participation and being part of the creative process.  Our
mission is to help everyone understand Open Source and to potentially
become part of that process.  There is a place for everyone in the
Open Source community whether you only want to use low cost, high
value software, to share in the community or just learn about
technology -- FOSUG is a non-profit organization there for you, to put
a human face on the Open Source community.
