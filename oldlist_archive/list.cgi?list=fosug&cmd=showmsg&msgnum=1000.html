Welcome to the Club. Get it!

I've been running jpilot when I do synch, which I don't do often enough. I
don't have a SD card yet so I'm not sure about how it synchs with stuff on
there. I'll defer to Drew on that.

I'm on Cingular and have been decently happy. I do wish the phone was a bit
louder. But with an earpiece, it's just fine. I've used the speaker phone on
occasion and it was decent. (Nothing even comes close to the clarity of my old
Moto i1000 on Nextel.) I just have a cheap earbud that came with my phone. My
phone was a refurb unit through Cingular.

As far as cases, I run a Rhinoskin. Picked it up at CompUSA for about $30,
IIRC. It's lived through some decent drops and smacks. But the door has a
tendancy to catch and pop open, especially entering and exiting vehicles. So I
usually take it off first. The belt clip portion is the week link. Mine has
worn out pretty good in 4 months. :( And watch the screw-in piece on the back
of the case. Make sure to check it. I used a quarter to tighten it (has a
round-type of slot for such things) and it't been great.

It does a few things I find goofy. The screen will stay on FOREVER when you
hang-up from a call, but shuts off too quickly when using Blazer (web
browser).

As far as Internet access, Cingular has a $20 unlimited plan, that's not
listed when you look at the Treo, and it's supporting services. But they'll
show the metered plans, which you'll bust after about 5 mins. on any normal
site. I check the weather radar on my phone when it's raining, and that's
about 70kB a shot!
