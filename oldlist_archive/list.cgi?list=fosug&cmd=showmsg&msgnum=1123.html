From http://tvtime.sourceforge.net/cards.htmlivtv

The ivtv driver supports cards that provide high quality MPEG2 encoded
video. This cards are ideal for PVR systems. However, tvtime has no MPEG2
decoding capabilities or audio playback code, and therefore cannot be used
to watch live TV from these cards.
Driver homepage: http://ivtv.sourceforge.net/
Assuming this is still true, I guess its MythTV time.  I suppose it can't be
any harder to set this up than beating my head against the wall trying to
configure something not designed to work in the first place.  Any words of
wisdom on MythTV, would be welcomed.
