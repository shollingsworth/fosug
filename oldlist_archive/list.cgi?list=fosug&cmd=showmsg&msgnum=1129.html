Stevo,

    Sigh. It was an unmitigated disaster. Drew, Patrick and Chad showed up, 
and Sanjiv was there as well, and I appreciated the support. What we didn't 
know at the outset, was that Linspire had emailed Bill Myers, FPCUG prez, 
that they weren't coming (thanks, Linspire - you have made my decision much 
simpler).
    We goaded Drew into considering doing the Linspire demo himself, since 
he had it in his bag of tricks that he brought to the meeting, but he needed 
internet access, and while his phone had that as an option, it wasn't set up 
for it at the moment. He tried, though, and got it up and running about the 
time the meeting ended.
    In the meantime, our Fearless Leader, Bill Myers, valiantly tried to 
salvage what was left of the evening by showing a demo CD of Microsoft 
Windows Defender and Windows Live One Care.
    Patrick and Drew contributed to the Q&A session very nicely, but I think 
Patrick and Chad got most of their entertainment out of watching Drew 
struggle to set up his internet line before we ran out of time.
    Although I didn't actually do it, the desire to bend over and hit my 
head repeatedly on the table out of embarassment and frustration was almost 
overwhelming.
    So, how was your evening?
