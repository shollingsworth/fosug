Dear All,

    Last night, somebody at the FPCUG (Fresno PC Users Group) meeting conned, er, asked Drew to give a demonstration of an installation of SuSe Linux, and some of the go-along software, for the August FPCUG meeting.

    With that in mind, I'd like to feature Linux and other open source topics in the FPCUG newsletter for that month. So, I am looking for articles connected to using Linux or other Open Source software that can be published or reprinted in the August RAM Pages.

    This is a good opportunity to promote the Open Source concept and give a plug for the FOSUG as well.

    I know that most of you guys aren't writers (code, maybe - paragraphs, not so much), but if you can write well enough to get your point across, I can smooth out any lumps during the editing process.

    Please email me if you have something you'd like to contribute or suggest. I generally need submissions by about the 22nd of the month before publication.
