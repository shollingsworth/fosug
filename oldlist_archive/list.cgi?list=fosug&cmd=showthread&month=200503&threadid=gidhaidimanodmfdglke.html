For free virus scanners for window$, off the top of my head you have:

AVG - http://free.grisoft.com/freeweb.php/doc/2/ (not OSS)
ClamWin (based off of clam av) - http://www.clamwin.com/ &
http://sourceforge.net/projects/clamwin/

I use ClamWin on my families windows computers.  Also, there's ClamMail
http://sourceforge.net/projects/clammail/ an email proxy based off of Clamav
for personal computers.  It will scan all email that comes through it for any
viruses before it gets to the user's inbox.  It's caught lots of stuff for me
on the systems I've installed it on.  (it even finds phishing emails.)
