      Great for looking up words on the fly or just seeing if the
spelling is right .. The Default location for the dictionary
is /usr/share/dict/words
 
Example
    
 bash-2.05$ look bigg

bigg
biggah
biggen
bigger
biggest
biggin
biggish
biggonet

You can also use aspell for checking the spelling inside files . Very
useful ....

Example
    
 bash-2.05$ aspell -c /path/to/file
