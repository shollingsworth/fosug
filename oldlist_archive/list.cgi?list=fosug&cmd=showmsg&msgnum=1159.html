Hey George,

Are you running all whizbang XGL + compiz goodness as well? I'm
currently running Ubuntu 6.06 (Dapper) and enabled it a couple weeks
ago.  The effects are REALLY DAMN COOL, though I do confess that since
I don't really utilize multiple desktops, I can't find a lot of
practical use for it, except for scale and the new switcher, which I
use regularly.  I do experience some high CPU utiliztion from time to
time, but no other glaring 'beta sotware' bugs.

For those of you who haven't seen the compiz demo video, there is a
link to it in the compiz wikipedia article:
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Compiz
Direct video link:
http://www.freedesktop.org/~davidr/xgl-demo1.xvid.avi

And for those with a fast connection and an ATI or nVidia 3d video
card that's not more than a couple years old, try downloading the
Kororaa live cd.  It includes all the cool features, and dosen't
require to futz with your system.  Find it at:
http://kororaa.org/static.php?page=static060318-181203#download
