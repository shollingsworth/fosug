Thanks, Robert!

Vulnerability reports are always good.

Reading it closer, the Denial Of Service apparently applies to Mambo
running on IIS, while apparently the "create-lots-of-files"
vulnerability applies to any server. 

Interestingly, Joomla has a fix while I still don't see one for Mambo.

This also brings the spotlight on the pink elephant about Mambo: The
Mambo/Joomla split, where most of the developers joined the Joomla fork.

I don't have the definitive answer to which branch is more likely to
last in the future, but I suspect it's Joomla. Incidentally, the main
reasons listed by the forkers appear to have been met (creation of the
Mambo Foundation and transfer of copyright) by now, but I don't expect
the two projects to merge back into one again...

I can't recall any other forks of open source projects in recent time.

Mattias
