I'm using D-Link PCMCIA card (DWL-G650) for my laptop, with ndiswrapper.
Works fine with Mandriva 2006. Since the card has the Atheros chip, I
could use madwifi but I haven't gotten around to installing it.

Definitely do your homework before purchasing wireless cards for Linux.
Same goes for modems and maybe other peripherals, too. I can't use the
color printer at the office because I can't find a driver for it.

Mattias
