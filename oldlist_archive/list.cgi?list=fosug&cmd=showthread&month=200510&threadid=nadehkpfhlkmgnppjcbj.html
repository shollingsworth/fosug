I can tell you that a $19 "500watt" and $24 "600watt" Powersupplys are
either crap or stolen.

I also noticed on the shipping weight. it's about 3lbs. 3lbs is simply not
enought weight to hold a well built 500+ watt p/s. Higher wattage p/s will
put out much more radient heat. Therefor requiring much more or larger heat
sinks. which are solid metal. The Average high quality 500+ p/s should
weight 5-6 lbs. Either these are not TRUE 500/600 watt, and they are using
gimiky math. (ei. 500watt "PEAK"). Or they are using the fans completly to
cool p/s this will be very loud, and if the fan fails. p/s will over heat.
Over heating p/s usually mean death to p/s and possibly other components
plugged into it. TOOO cheap
