There's a lot more to Open Source than Linux and free software.  Open
Source is about participation and being part of the creative process
-- you need not be a programmer.  FOSUG is a non-profit organization
whose mission is to help everyone benefit from Open Source and to
potentialy become part of the creative process.  There is a place for
everyone in the Open Source community wheather you want low cost high
value software, to share in the community or just learn about
technology -- FOSUG is there for you to give a helping hand and put a
human face on Open Source.
