Nice to see you here, Suzi!

I've been admiring your work on a number of community web sites, as well
as YOUR OWN... That little "what we do" animation on thinkinkless.com is
funny!

I'm a web developer myself - not much of a graphical designer but I work
with the "back-end" side of web applications or web sites, in PHP/MySQL
mostly. Been developing a web application for "a long while" now - the
online demo is coming any week now...

Drupal? I'm afraid I haven't used it, since I built my company web site
(http://www.activeagenda.net) with Mambo. Drupal is possibly more
extensible/flexible than Mambo/Joomla. However, the availability of
attractive, well-designed templates is very different: I couldn't find
almost any for Drupal, while there are plenty of useful and tweakable
templates for Mambo/Joomla.  Also, most Drupal sites that I have seen
seem to be kind of hard to navigate - as a user, it's easy to feel lost
or not realize what else there is to see on the site.

I'll try to make it to the next presentation.
