Does the Powerchute software run on Linux? And does the USB connector
work easily with Linux? The BE500R, for instance comes with USB only...

On a side note, I wonder why desktop/server power supplies don't just
come with battery backups in them? The computer runs internally on DC
(9V?), the batteries provide DC. This all seems to work fine on laptops
(when the batteries work, that is). A UPS has to go through the extra
conversion from the battery DC power to AC output. (Only to be converted
back by the computer's own power supply).

Maybe internal battery UPS's would deliver longer uptime and less
complicated technology since everything would be DC and low-voltage? Do
they exist at all?
