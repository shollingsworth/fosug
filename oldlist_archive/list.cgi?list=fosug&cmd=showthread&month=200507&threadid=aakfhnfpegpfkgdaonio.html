My office is located near the Manchester Mall which is one of FAX's (I
always through Fresno Area Rapid Transit had a much better ring) major
hubs, so almost every morning, when I'm turning off of shields onto
Blackstone, I'm stuck behind a bus.  Every couple of day's I'm behind
a bus with an advertisement for 'Line-X' spray on truck bedliners,
with the word Line-X displayed prominently in a colored oval.

Now, it's usually really early, and I'm not a morning person, (That's
my excuse and I'm sticking to it) but I always find myself really
excited for like .75 seconds while I think, "Cool, a company
advertising LINUX in Fresno, I wonder what that's about . . .".  Then

the SAME F-ing advert.

Just thought I'd share my idiocy with the community.
