Are you other TV picking up those stations satisfactorally? Could be a low
signal strength to your house. Does it change depending on the time of day? If
everything is having problems, you could give Comcast a call and complain.
They'll come out and check your signal stength, and if it's low, install an
amp.

If you put your own amp in look at the Motorola one. Supposed to be the best.
About 44 or so shipped via Amazon. It's 99 list. Install it at the entry point
into your home and split EVERYTHING there, if you can.
