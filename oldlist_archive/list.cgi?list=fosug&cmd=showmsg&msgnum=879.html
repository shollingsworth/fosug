Here's what the manual says:

Local Console Management
You can manage the GigaSwitch locally by connecting the switch to a PC 
or workstation with terminal emulation software using the serial port. 
Before you log on to the GigaSwitch, make sure that the switch’s serial 
port settings are as follows:
BAUD RATE: 115200
FLOW: None
PARITY: None
STOP BITS: 1
DATA BITS: 8
EMULATION: ANSI
CURSOR EMULATION: VT100
