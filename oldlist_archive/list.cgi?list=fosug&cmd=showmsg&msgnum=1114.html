Butterfield's still exists, but under the new name Sequoia Brewing. The
menu is much the same, and the beer is still mediocre. It's still a nice
place to hang out, and their IPA isn't undrinkable.

However, for the best micro-brew in town, there's Full Circle in
Chinatown (on F near Ventura). Not a dinner venue, since they serve just
beer and wines that they make.

Livingstone's is the restaurant next to Starline. A popular hangout
that's been in the same location for many years.

Million Elephant is a new Thai/Lao restaurant in the space that was
previously J-Ho's, and Brix before that. Very friendly service, good food.
