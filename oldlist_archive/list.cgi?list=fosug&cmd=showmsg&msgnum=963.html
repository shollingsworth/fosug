*nod*  When in this situation, I keep the whole toolchain on an NFS mount.  I
turn it on when I need to build and then off when I don't.  Export the
relevant bits into the shell (a la build a .mybuildsh file on the nfs
partition that you source before starting your builds).  This may be slower
than usual, but it gets the job done.  In the case you have where you're doing
screenings, I can't advise the other method of just compiling everything to
386 compat mode.
