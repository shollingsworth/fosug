=====Suggestion Box

If anybody has any suggestions in regards to what they think would improve 
the group, and the prospects of new members, as well as keeping
the ones we have now, pipe up, we'd love to hear what you have to say.
==========

    I am the new editor of the Fresno PC Users Group newsletter, "The RAM 
Pages". In May, our general meeting will be about "Linspire", a flavor of 
Linux. I'd really like to have some input by way of an article from this 
group on what you like about Linux in general, how Linux has become easier 
to install/use, the wide range of applications available for home and 
business. . .anything that can spark the group's interest in any Linux 
distribution.
    If I can wrangle an invitation, I'd also like to sit in on your meeting 
this month, if you haven't already had it. . .
