An oldie but goodie.  You may not have enough machine for full blown Linux.
A more minimal GUI for instance may be called for.  IMHO I'd try Puppy Linux
or Damn Small Linux on that box.  They both have GUI's more in keeping with
your horsepower.  Older high end graphics cards can also cause issues unless
treated as generic VESA.  The rest of the group has considerably more Linux
experience than I and may have other suggestions.

Have a great day -- George
