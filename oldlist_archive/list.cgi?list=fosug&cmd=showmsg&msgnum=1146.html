Hi all.
Please allow me to remind you that on this Wednesday, May/10, Tim Huckaby 
will be presenting at our monthly .Net User Group meeting.
It is 100% free and you are encouraged to bring as many developers and you’d 
like. There will be pizza and refreshments for all, also free. Additionally, 
we’ll be giving away thousands of dollars worth of prizes (bring a business 
card!).

There is a small correction from my previous email. Tim’s presentation will 
include WWF but it won’t be the main topic. He will be talking primarily 
about smart client development. Both current techniques and what’s coming up 
with Windows Vista such as WPF (Windows Presentation Foundation, previously 
known as Avalon)

Bill Gates will be presenting a killer demo at the WinHEC 
(http://www.microsoft.com/whdc/winhec) in a couple of weeks on WPF, Office 
12 and OSS (Open Source Software). You don’t need to attend WinHEC to see 
it. Tim will be presenting the same exact demo to us first hand, 2 weeks 
before Bill shows it to the world!!!

Don't forget:
Wednesday May/10
Fresno State's University Business Center Room 194
6:00pm

For more information: www.centralcaldotnet.com

Thanks
