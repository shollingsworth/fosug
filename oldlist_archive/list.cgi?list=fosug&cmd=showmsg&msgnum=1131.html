Hey Drew,

Seeing your laptop yesterday has really got me considering getting a
new one.  Yours seemd like it was a great size.  Just small enough to
be portable, but not so small as to be un-useable.  If you don't mind
my asking, about what are the dimension of the computer, and what is
the size of the screen.  I'd like to use it as a starting point for my
potential purchase.

For everyone else, what are your reccomendations/experiences regarding
a good portable computer.  Bellow is a (possibly incomplete) list of
what I think my requirements are:

Requirements:
1. Good portablity / lightweight.  I want something I can put in a
backback or messenger bag without it being obvious I've got a computer
in there.
2. Good battery life.  I really would like a solid 3 hours application
usage without wireless.
3. Decent Linux support.  I don't need any exotic hardware it may have
to work (ie, jog dial, or softkeyboard buttons), but X support for the
videocard would be a real bonus.
4. Integrated wireless that works at least using NDIS Wrapper
5. Onboard ethernet without a dongle.

Nice To Have but not necessesary:
1. Touchpad.  I HATE NUB POINTERS
2. Onboard optical drive.  Willing to sacrifice this for better
battery life / smaller form factor
3. Availabe PCMCIA slot

Oh yeah, and it has to be less than $500 USD.  Just kidding.

Thanks for your time.
