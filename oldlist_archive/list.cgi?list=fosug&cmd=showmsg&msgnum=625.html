SuSE does a great job of recognizing a wide variety of USB devices
automatically.  This include a number of different card readers, thumb
drives and digital cameras.  It does a great job on the desktop with
KDE.  Configuration issues are minimal, especially once I learned to
trust YaST.  I have limited experience with other distros except
Linspire and Puppy but My satisfaction level with SuSE 10.0 and its
predecessors doesn't motivate me to change.
