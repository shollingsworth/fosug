I have to really give the Gentoo crowd credit however.  That have taken 95

Yes once you get to know gentoo ahead of time you would know the correct

prehand.  Even with a GUI install I still would not push gentoo at the
begining linux user. Granted it's extreamly powerfull, but in the wrong
hands it's very much like giving a baby a 2000HP Trike.
