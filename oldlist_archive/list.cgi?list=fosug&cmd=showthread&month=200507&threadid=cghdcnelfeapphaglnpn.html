Two steps led to an enjoyable viewing experience. First I upgraded to the 
latest xine RPMs on http://cambuca.ldhs.cetuc.puc-rio.br/xine/ and second I 
found the place in in kmplayer for picking which viewer to use. It had been 
set to mplayer which still plays the French speech track. I changed it to 
xine and with the new RPMs got English. Without kmplayer, xine refused to 
show the movie and stated that decrypting DVDs in some countries is illegal. 
The right combination did however allow me to view the new DVD I legally 
paid for. Linux is a puzzle with many pieces that can be solved with 
patience and stubbornness -- I love it.
