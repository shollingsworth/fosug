The reason that DSL had to be shared was because that POTS copper was 
installed before deregulation. That copper wire belongs to everyone. RBOCs 
never had to share copper or fiber they installed after deregulation. For 
those who may not be familiar with telephony acronyms, POTS is Plain Old 
Telephone System.

I forgot how long ago the AT&T breakup was. We went from the Ma Bell 
monopoly that does all to separate regional bell operating companies, i.e. 
Pacific Bell (now part of SBC), and long distance services from AT&T and 
MCI. Western Electric and Bell Labs were part of AT&T and were also spun off 
-- think Lucent. For all practical purposes that deregulation and AT&T 
breakup gave birth to the telecommunications industry we have today.
