a new position at Microsoft on 23 May 2005. According to drobbins:

projects." While in the midst of hastily packing to move to Redmond,
drobbins nonetheless managed to find the time to finalize the transfer
of Gentoo's intellectual property (essentially copyrights on ebuilds
and other software as well as soon-to-be trademarked Gentoo logos) to
the not-for-profit Gentoo Foundation, Inc.
